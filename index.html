VTec Virus

What is VTec?

VTec is a bacterium that causes diarrhoea and producesa toxin which can damage your bowel wall. E.coli O157 is the best known strain. People generally become ill 2-4 days after being infected but it can take longer for others. Most cases occur in the summer and children under ten catch it more easily than other age groups.

How did I get infected?

  • Contact with farm animals.
  • Eating food/drink contaminated with even tiny amounts of faeces.
  • Person to person spread.
  • Untreated water from private wells.

    • Symptoms

  • Diarrhoea is usually the first symptom.
  • Vomiting.
  • Inflamation of the bowel.

    • How to prevent it?

    Washing hands regularly is the only way to prevent it.
